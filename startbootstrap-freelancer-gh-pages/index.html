About

Urban Food Justice is an action research project to inform responsive policy making. Food justice aims to ensure that the benefits and risks of producing, distributing, and consuming food are shared fairly by everyone involved and to transform the food system to eliminate inequities.


Whether you're a student, a working adult, or an elderly citizen you deserve the right to food sovereignty.
